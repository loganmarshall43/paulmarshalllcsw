About Me

I am a licensed clinical social worker with over 25+ years experience. I am a certified EMDR therapist and an EMDRIA approved consultant. My experience is in rural Utah. Working as a rural therapist has its ups and downs. The downside is there is little support as a provider. The upside is I have treated many disorders across the lifespan. My experience is rich because i have had to learn and grow along the way to be an effective provider for those seeking services. My EMDR journey has been just that, a journey. I was lost for a while then I took a training from Deany Laliotis and started consultation. For the last four years that has been my mission and i am now a competent EMDR therapist. I love the outdoors, hiking and camping. I serve all ages. Currently licensed in Utah and Virginia.
